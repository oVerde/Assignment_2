Casa Lubra

Casa Lubra was a project developed in 2024 with the goal of renewing the company's visual identity.

It is a local store dedicated to selling home comfort products, such as bed linens, towels, and similar items.

With the intention of conveying comfort and quality, I proposed a new identity that involved a complete redesign of the logo and color palette. The only retained feature from the previous identity was the logo font, inspired by Bauhaus typography, to preserve a sense of heritage associated with the brand. This choice also ensured the change was not too aggressive, considering the public's familiarity with the existing identity.
