Vernovo

Vernovo presents a fresh and approachable visual identity, reflecting the project’s goal of making design services accessible and friendly. The concept emphasizes a modern, human-centered approach to creative work.

The brand identity uses shades of green as a playful nod to the name itself – "Ver Novo" – combining the idea of seeing (Ver) and renewal (Novo). This color palette conveys growth, freshness, and positivity.

A central element of the identity is the eye, symbolizing observation, attention to detail, and awareness. It represents the idea that design starts with seeing and understanding the world carefully.

Overall, Vernovo’s visual identity balances friendliness and professionalism, combining colors, symbols, and a concept that communicates openness, creativity, and thoughtful observation.

Vernovo is a collaborative freelance project and is not associated with any company. All services provided are independent and do not represent competition with other brands. For any inquiries or support, the official project website is available below.
