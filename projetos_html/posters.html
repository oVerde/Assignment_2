Dynamic Posters

Dynamic Posters was an experimental project I developed with the aim of exploring my ability to create posters quickly and intuitively. Over a certain period of time, I challenged myself to dedicate no more than one hour to each poster, without a defined theme or conceptual restrictions. The idea was simple: to practice pure creative expression, experimenting with compositions, colors, and typography without overloading the process with excessive planning. The result is a spontaneous collection of graphic pieces, created essentially for the sake of art itself, reflecting both the freedom of the exercise and the visual diversity achieved.
